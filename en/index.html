Who I am

I am Jerome Kwamo, founder of JKK Jerome — a visual production and digital marketing practice based in Hamburg, Germany, working exclusively in the boating industry. I work with boat dealers, shipyards, charter companies and brokers who need more than a digital presence: they need to be found, remembered and chosen.

My approach combines cinematic film, high end photography and marketing strategy powered by artificial intelligence. I am not a generalist agency. Every project I take on belongs to the water — boats, yachts, sailing yachts, motorboats, catamarans. That specialisation lets me understand the market, speak its visual language and produce material that actually converts.

Services for the marine sector

Professional marine photography — Photo sessions on board, in the marina, under way and at the yard. Product photography for digital catalogues, broker listings and social media. I work with natural light and cinematic techniques to present every boat as what it is: a singular piece.

Film and video production — Brand films, virtual walkthroughs, social content and editorial pieces. From short spots for Instagram and YouTube to shipyard documentaries. Film is the most powerful tool to convey the experience of being on the water before a buyer ever steps on board.

AI visuals — I use artificial intelligence to create visualisations, image variations and digital showroom material for boats and yachts. AI does not replace creativity; it amplifies it. Every piece passes through human review before it is published.

AI sales — Automated enquiry handling, intelligent chat assistants and AI supported sales communication for dealers and charter companies. Interested buyers get answers immediately — even at night and on weekends — and you receive qualified contacts instead of cold traffic.

Social media and content — Content strategy for Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook and YouTube. Planning, production and community management built to generate real enquiries, not just followers. Content made for the marine buyer, not for a generic algorithm.

Online advertising and visibility — Google Ads, Meta Ads and technical SEO for the marine sector. My goal: when someone in London, Amsterdam, Palma or Fort Lauderdale searches for a boat, your company shows up first. I work with realistic budgets and clear metrics.

Websites and digital showrooms — Design and development of websites for dealers, shipyards and brokers. Fast pages, optimised for search engines and built to turn visitors into qualified contacts — including inventory integration, smart forms and behaviour analytics.

Sales systems — Digital sales processes that connect your inventory, your enquiries and your follow up into one clear flow. Less manual administration, fewer lost contacts, more closed deals.

Consulting — Strategy sessions for dealers and shipyards that want to digitalise their marketing and sales. I analyse your current presence, identify the gaps and give you a concrete, prioritised plan you can execute.

An international market

The boating industry is international by nature. Buyers research in English, boats change flags and owners across borders, and a yacht built on the Baltic is sold on the Mediterranean. From the marinas of the Solent and the Dutch coast to Mallorca, the Adriatic and the Gulf, there is real demand for professional marine marketing that works across markets.

Many dealers and shipyards still rely on printed catalogues, generic third party portal listings and social media without a strategy. My work closes that gap: I bring the visual quality and digital marketing precision that already performs in the German and Nordic market to clients worldwide.

I am based in Hamburg, but I produce anywhere in the world. I have photographed and filmed in ports on the Mediterranean, the Atlantic and the Baltic. Distance is not an obstacle — a lack of digital visibility is.
